WHAT WE DO

  • We help local neighbourhood communities clean up recyclables and rubbish to keep footpaths and roads clean.

  • We work with local governments to ensure that the suburbs are monitored and cleaned regularly.

  • We engage in monthly cleaning parades with the local neigbourhoods (7th of every month). Your support means everything!
